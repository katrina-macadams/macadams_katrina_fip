Our Story

The Art of Visibility

Our bespoke frosted glass bottles are a canvas for creativity and clarity, intricately designed to reveal the vibrant spectacle within. Strategic clear glass cutouts showcase the colorful swirl of liquid and orbs, inviting a visual dance that complements the drink’s unique character. Every bottle of Orbitz not only refreshes but also captivates the eye, making each sip an engaging experience.

Redefined

Levitating Flavours Redefined

The floating orbs are not just a feast for the eyes but a revolution in flavour and texture. By leveraging culinary physics, and addressing past concerns we enhanced both their flavour profile and mouthfeel. Each orb slowly floats through the beverage, an invitation to explore a futuristic drinking experience.
